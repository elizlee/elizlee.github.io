About

Thanks for stopping by!

I am currently a master's student in the computational linguistics program at the University of Rochester. Broadly my interests are in machine translation, NLP, semantics, and second-language acquisition. I hope to use the knowledge I gain to work on statistical MT systems or help develop software designed to assist in second-language learning.

Aside from computational linguistics, my interests include coding, fiction writing, video games, and bubble tea.

I can be contacted at elgclee@gmail.com or elee86@ur.rochester.edu.
