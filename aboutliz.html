About

Thanks for stopping by!

I am currently a second-year master's student in the computational linguistics program at the University of Rochester and a research assistant for the Quantitative Semantics Lab. Broadly my interests are in natural language processing, machine learning, software development, data analysis, semantics, and second-language acquisition. I also hope to become proficient in Japanese.

Aside from all things computational and linguistic, my interests include fiction writing, video games, and bubble tea.
