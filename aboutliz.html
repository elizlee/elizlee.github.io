About

Thanks for stopping by!

My most recent major accomplishment was completing my M.S. program in Computational Linguistics at the University of Rochester. During my time there, I took courses in linguistics and computer science while assisting with research for the Quantitative Semantics Lab and the TRIPS Lab.

Broadly my interests are in natural language processing, artificial intelligence, software development, data analysis, semantics, and second-language acquisition. I also hope to become proficient in Japanese.

Aside from all things computational and linguistic, my interests include fiction writing, video games, and bubble tea.

Currently I am searching for a full-time job while continuing to participate in the Quantitative Semantics Lab and using what I have learned in school to work on personal projects.
