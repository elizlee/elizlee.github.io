About

Thanks for stopping by!

I am currently a first-year master's student in the computational linguistics program at the University of Rochester. Broadly my interests are in machine translation, NLP, machine learning, semantics, and second-language acquisition. I hope to use the knowledge I gain to work on neural MT systems or help develop software designed to assist in second-language learning.

Aside from computational linguistics, my interests include fiction writing, video games, and bubble tea.

(You may notice that there is some Japanese on this website. If you can read Japanese, you may also notice that I am not fluent in Japanese. I am trying to keep up my abilities in this language, so if you would like to point out any mistakes you see, I would greatly appreciate the feedback!)
